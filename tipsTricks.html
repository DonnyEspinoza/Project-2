Tips and Tricks


What are the 3 Core skills to be a Good Player?

In an objective and team based game where outplaying your opponent is incredibly important to winning gunfights, sometimes it requires the use of rush routes to get from point A to B and predictions from where you think your opponent is coming from to hold an angle behind cover. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  1. Know the map
  2. Practice moving fluently through common rush routes
  3. Enemies always respawn opposite from your furthest teammate
  4. Avoid common choke areas
  5. Know the objective locations
  6. Know the different map callouts

Movement and Positions are KEY to beating your enemy to a given point on the map. Power positions are usually one of the deciding factors that determine the outcome of a fight, so here are some things to try and remember:

  1. Always be moving even when behind cover (A moving target is harder to hit than a stationary one)
  2. Try to avoid peeking common angles
  3. Don't be afraid to disengage from a fight when you know you're going to lose, it's better to repositions and try to reapproach from a different angle
  4. Rotational aim assist only applies when the player character is in motion

Always being ready for a gunfight is an important part of clearing buildings and pushing objectives. A player always needs to be on guard and prepared for a fight. Some common strategies are:

  1. Always have your gun up and reloaded
  2. Center your crosshair at common areas
  3. check ALL your corners for any stragglers
  4. Moving with your teammates as a group is the best way to make sure you can't be caught off guard
  5. If your aim is off, don't disengage and try to push through a different route
